**Different Things** - a mod that adds the «Cruiser» and «Bulldog» vehicles to the game's shops, as well as expands the player's arsenal with new types of weapons and gadgets. Additionally, the mod makes some balance and gameplay tweaks, and increases enemy variety.

**Author - MrKarfen**

<summary><h2>The full changelog</h2></summary>

## Changes introduced by Different Things

### Vehicles:
* Added the Bulldog vehicle. Has two cabins and three cargoes. Can be bought in cities in the Krai, Ridzin, and Vaterland locations.
* Added the Stingray vehicle (not available to the player). Has one cabin, only available for enemies in the second region (Librium and Argen).
* Added the Cruiser vehicle to shops. Three cabins and three cargoes are available. Can be bought in cities in the Sacred Grove, Librium, Argen, and Vahat.
* Added a new Ural cargo variant for enemies (not available to the player).

### Weapons:
* The "Bumblebee" and "Omega" mortars have been brought to a playable state.
* Added 10 new weapon types:

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 1. American Hornet (machine gun for the small slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 2. PKTU (machine gun for the small slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 3. Pecheneg (machine gun for the small slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 4. Imp (cannon for the small slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 5. Gimlet (rocket launcher for the small slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 6. Browning (machine gun for the medium slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 7. Bastard (shotgun for the medium slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 8. Ares (plasma cannon for the medium slot, based on the Auto-Stop from "Hard Truck Apocalypse: Rise of Clans")

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 9. Bofors (anti-aircraft autocannon for the giant slot)

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp; 10. Trapper (rocket launcher for the giant slot)

### Other:
* Added Anti-Missile Defense to shops.
* Made some changes have been made to the weapon/transport models.
* Made some changes have been made to the balance.

### Changes introduced by the "More phrases during combat" option <ins>[Russian version only]</ins>
* Added new lines for the protagonist during combat.

### Changes introduced by the "Authentic names" option <ins>[Russian version only]</ins>
* Replaced some names and titles (mainly concerning bartenders and dynamic bandits) with ones more fitting for each region. Added names to bartenders who didn't have them.

### Changes introduced by the "Barmen sell area maps" option
* Added the ability to buy area maps from barmens.

### Changes introduced by the "Racing Bulldog" option
* The vehicle in Vahat races is replaced with the Bulldog.

### Changes introduced by the "Varied enemies" option
* Some "Vagabonds" and "Nomads" in the second region (Librium and Argen) are replaced with "Bounty hunters" (a faction cut from the original game).
* All "Vagabonds" and "Nomads" in Vahat are replaced with "Kurtadam" (a faction cut from the original game).
* The encyclopedia description of the "Kurtadam" faction has been slightly expanded.

## Download
The actual version of the modification can be downloaded on the [releases page](https://github.com/Comrade-Karfen/DifferentThings/releases).

## Installation
1. Install Community Remaster with all additional options.
2. In DEM Community Mod Manager, on the "Mods" tab, click "Add mod" and select the DifferentThings archive.
3. After it appears in the mod list, click "Unpack".
4. After unpacking, click "Install" and follow the mod manager's instructions.

## Modifications compatible with Different Things

### Fully compatible:
* Improved Storyline [Separate version during installation]
* Absque nota [Separate version during installation]
* Extra Machines
* Textures Update
* Rusty Interface

### Partially compatible (can be installed, but minor issues are likely):
* OverPhys

### Adaptations for the following mods are planned for the future:
* Ex Machina: Upgrade
* kto mod Remastered

> [!WARNING]
> *Installing Different Things over modifications not listed as compatible is not supported, therefore technical support will not be provided in such cases. You can always ask me to adapt Different Things to a specific mod, and I will consider the proposal. Full adaptation of the mod for **Hard Truck Apocalypse: Rise of Clans** or **Ex Machina: Arcade** will never happen.*
