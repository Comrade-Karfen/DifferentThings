# Changes introduced by Different Things
---
### Vehicles:
* Added the Bulldog vehicle. Has two cabins and three cargoes. Can be bought in cities in the Krai, Ridzin, and Vaterland locations.
* Added the Stingray vehicle (not available to the player). Has one cabin, only available for enemies in the second region (Librium and Argen).
* Added the Cruiser vehicle to shops. Three cabins and three cargoes are available. Can be bought in cities in the Sacred Grove, Librium, Argen, and Vahat.
* Added a new Ural cargo variant for enemies (not available to the player).

### Weapons:
