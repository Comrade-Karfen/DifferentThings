# Modifications compatible with Different Things

### Fully compatible:
* Improved Storyline [Separate version during installation]
* Absque nota [Separate version during installation]
* Extra Machines
* Textures Update
* Rusty Interface

### Partially compatible (can be installed, but minor issues are likely):
* OverPhys

### Adaptations for the following mods are planned for the future:
* Ex Machina: Upgrade
* kto mod Remastered

> *Installing Different Things over modifications not listed as compatible is not supported, therefore technical support will not be provided in such cases. You can always ask me to adapt Different Things to a specific mod, and I will consider the proposal. Full adaptation of the mod for **Hard Truck Apocalypse: Rise of Clans** or **Ex Machina: Arcade** will never happen.*

## Useful information

### New console commands have been added to the game:
* givefuel () - completely refuels the player's car.

* givehp () - completely repairs the player's car.
